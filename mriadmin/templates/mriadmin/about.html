ABOUT

The Mount Royal International School in Dehiwala was started in 2011, as a business unit of I.B.S. Campus (Pvt) Ltd. Initially the Preschool was started and two years later upper grades from 1 to 11 were started.

Some facts about the School:

  • The Vision of the school is “To create a value based society with meaningful living”
  • The Mission of the school is “ To provide learning experience based on the divine knowledge and acquired knowledge that will advance the holistic development of our students”
  • Our Core Values are “Discipline, Honesty, Peace and Humanity”
  • The school has three separate sections, namely; the Pre-school, Boys school and the Girls school which are located at three separate places / buildings on the same Kawdana road.
  • “Value based education” is the basic principle of this school. The school strives to inculcate good values in the minds of its students.
  • The school maintains a facebook account to share its activities with parents and stakeholders.
  • Students from all communities in this country and some foreign students, mainly from Maldives study at this school
  • The school has made four houses, namely Ibn Sina, Umar, Thomas Alva Edison, and Albert Einstein to hold competitions and events such as sports meet, debate competitions etc
  • The principle of this school Mr. T.L.M. Jemseed, who is a Chartered Accountant by profession and having a BCom and MBA degrees. Also he is the Chairman of Benhill College in Gampola.
